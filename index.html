Life at Blooming Shoots

We offer a holistic learning experience that balances the CBC curriculum with world-class talent development.

CBC/CBE Curriculum

Fully compliant with the Competency Based Education system, focusing on 21st-century skills and practical learning for Playgroup to Grade 5.

Chess & Mind Games

Developing strategic thinking and concentration through our weekly chess classes.

Music & Arts

Unlocking creativity through musical instruments, voice coaching, and creative arts.

French Lessons

Giving our pupils a global edge with foreign language introduction from an early age.

Swimming

Professional swimming lessons in a safe environment to build physical fitness and water safety skills.

Skating

Fun and energetic skating sessions that help improve balance, coordination, and physical agility.

Parent Essentials

Reliable Transportation

Safe and punctual school bus services picking and dropping children within Kasarani, Mwiki, and surrounding areas.

Nutritious Feeding Program

We provide balanced, delicious, and hot meals daily to ensure our pupils have the energy they need to learn and grow.
