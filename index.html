Who We Are

Welcome to Graceful Rose Nail Spa, where beauty meets affordability in the heart of Hunters, Kasarani. We believe that everyone deserves to feel pampered without breaking the bank.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality nail care in a clean, professional, and relaxing environment. Whether you're looking for a quick polish change or a full set of stunning acrylics, our dedicated team is here to ensure you leave feeling confident and refreshed.

Quality

We use top-tier products to ensure your nails stay healthy and vibrant.

Passion

Our technicians are artists who take pride in every single stroke.

At Graceful Rose, you're not just a client—you're part of our family. Come experience the "Graceful Rose" today!
